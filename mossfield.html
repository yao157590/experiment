MOSSFIELD FARMS

A digital journey into sustainable agriculture, showcasing the beauty of organic farming through immersive storytelling and Framer-powered interactions.

Technologies
Framer, React, GSAP

Cultivating Digital Roots: The Mossfield Organic Farms website was designed to bridge the gap between rural agriculture and urban consumers. Studio Mossfield utilized an earthy color palette and fluid, organic scrolling animations to mimic the natural rhythms of the seasons.

By leveraging Framer's powerful component system, the team created a seamless, non-profit digital experience that educates users on regenerative farming practices while driving community supported agriculture (CSA) sign-ups.
