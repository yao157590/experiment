CYAN SIGNATURES

Interactive Flavor Profiles & Menu Design

Cyan Nebula

Vibrant & Crisp

A breathtaking digital signature drink featuring a layered blend of blue raspberry infusion, sparkling botanical tonic, and a sharp hit of fresh lime citrus. Served over hand-crushed glowing ice matrices to capture light reflections perfectly in WebGL engines.

Palette: Sweet Tart, Neon Berry, Citrus Zing
Visual Density: Gradient Shift (Deep Indigo to Cyan)
Key Notes: Blue Raspberry, Botanical Tonic, Lime Core

Electric Velvet

Smooth & Creamy

A rich, smooth liquid simulation designed to showcase dense fluid weights. This drink layers cold-pressed mountain blueberry nectar with clear white cranberry extract, finished with a floated layer of aerated sweet vanilla cream that holds its structure dynamically.

Palette: Creamy Vanilla, Velvet Berry, Subtle Sweet
Visual Density: High Contrast Opaque Layering
Key Notes: Wild Blueberry, White Cranberry, Vanilla Foam

The Glitch

Sour & Textured

An experimental blend engineered for maximum sensory contrast. A base of electric green apple acid drops combined with bright cyan citrus syrup, topped off with crackling sugar crystals that dance across the glass surface using specialized micro-particle physics.

Palette: Extreme Sour, Green Apple, Popping Sugar
Visual Density: Particle-Heavy Translucent Green
Key Notes: Green Apple Tart, Blue Citrus, Fizz Crystals

Solar Flare Mocktail

Warm & Effervescent

A warm counterpoint to the cool cyan palette. This recipe features zero-proof fiery ginger root extract, blood orange juice, and sparkling mineral water. The app simulates real-time temperature heatmaps using a glowing orange-to-pink visual gradient glow.

Palette: Ginger Spice, Citrus Blood Orange, Crisp Carbonation
Visual Density: Effervescent, Active Bubbles
Key Notes: Fiery Ginger, Blood Orange, Sparkling Mineral
