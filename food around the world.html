Culinary Regions & Traditions

UMAMI
East Asia · Balance & Technique
The Art of Wok Hei & Precision

Rooted heavily in the harmony of five core flavors. Key emphasis rests on temperature control, seasonal components, and intricate fermentation methods like aged soy and misos.

Staples: Ramen, Dim Sum, Kimchi, Sushi
OLIVE
Mediterranean · Simplicity & Freshness
Coastal Sun & Botanical Oils

Celebrating simple base structures elevated by pristine ingredients. Olive oils, wild citrus, stone-baked grain doughs, and fresh sea proteins form the pillars of this coastal diet.

Staples: Neapolitan Pizza, Mezze, Paella, Gelato
SPICE
South Asia · Complexity & Infusion
The Chemistry of Masala Grids

A masterclass in layering aromatics. Utilizing dry-roasting techniques to release essential oils from cardamom, turmeric, and cumin, producing deep, rich sauce reductions.

Staples: Biryani, Tandoori, Tarka Dal, Naan
MAIZE
Mesoamerica · Earth & Nixtamalization
Volcanic Terroir & Ancestral Corn

Built upon an ancient trinity of corn, beans, and squash. The complex scientific process of nixtamalization transforms raw grains into supple doughs paired with smoky chili variations.

Staples: Tacos al Pastor, Mole Poblano, Tamales
