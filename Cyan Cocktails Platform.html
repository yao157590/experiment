CYAN COCKTAILS

8.88
Score

A sleek, dark-mode application redefining digital mixology through stunning WebGL liquid simulations and an intuitive interactive interface.

Technologies
Vue.js, Three.js, WebGL

Pouring Pixels: Cyan Cocktails elevates the digital beverage experience by combining a moody, neon-accented dark mode with cutting-edge 3D rendering. The agency, Cyan Lab, focused on micro-interactions that make the app feel incredibly tactile and responsive.

By utilizing Three.js and custom WebGL shaders, the platform features real-time liquid physics, allowing users to visually construct their drinks layer by layer before saving them to their personal collections.
